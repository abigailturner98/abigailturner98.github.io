Welcome!


Carolina Style Dance Company provides dedicated and experienced dancers with the opportunity to perform for the Chapel Hill community and share their love for different forms of dance through tap, jazz, and ballet.

Carolina Style is entirely student-led and student choreographed. We have auditions in the fall and spring for each of our separate companies and we come together for two performances throughout the year. These performances include a benefit concert in the fall and our showcase at Memorial Hall in the spring. Members can be a part of any or all groups based on interest and availability. Each group meets for 2 hours every week for rehearsal, depending on choreographer preferences and needs. We also have some really fun social, volunteer, and promotional events planned as well!

Please send an email to the primary contact (Claire Bartel, bartelc@live.unc.edu) with any questions you may have, including information about our audition process, rehearsal schedule, performances, etc. Check out our social media too, to stay informed on performance and audition dates!
